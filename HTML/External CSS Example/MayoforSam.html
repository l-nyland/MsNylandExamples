Mayo for Sam: Mayo GAA

Mayo is the best football team in the country. Their colours are Red and Green.

Mayo have won the All-Ireland Senior Football competition in 1936, 1950 and 1951. It has been x years since their last All-Ireland win, but they have appeared in 10 finals since 1996.

The Curse

Since the 1990s, a sports curse story had circulated that Mayo would not win another All-Ireland until all members of the 1951 team had died. The supposed cause was that the 1951 champions, returning through Foxford in a coach after the win, passed a funeral and did not respectfully stop their celebrations, and were cursed by the priest. The curse claimed that Mayo would not win another All-Ireland title while the members of the winning team were still alive. They have infamously bottled many games since.
